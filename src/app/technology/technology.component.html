PGT-A Testing:

Maximize your chances of a successful pregnancy with Preimplantation Genetic Testing for Aneuploidy (PGT-A) testing. Our advanced genetic screening assesses embryos for chromosomal abnormalities. Reduce the risk of miscarriage and increase the likelihood of a healthy birth. Embrace peace of mind as you embark on your journey to parenthood.

PGT-M Testing:

For families facing inherited genetic conditions, Gattaca Genomics offers Preimplantation Genetic Testing for Monogenic Disorders (PGT-M) testing. Safeguard your child from genetic disorders by identifying embryos that are non-carriers for some known detrimental genetic mutations. Empower your family’s future with careful, well-informed family planning.

This is a list of diseases that can be screened for using PGT-M:


- Cystic Fibrosis

- Fragile-X syndrome

- Muscular dystrophy

- Huntington’s disease and many more.

Euploid Embryo

An euploid embryo refers to an embryo that has a complete, balanced set of chromosomes, with the correct number of chromosomes in each pair. In humans, euploid embryos typically have 46 chromosomes, with 23 pairs, and this includes one pair of sex chromosomes (XX for females and XY for males).
